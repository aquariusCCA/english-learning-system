# 否定

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **一句話核心**
> 否定不只靠 not。**否定字**（no／never／hardly／seldom／few／little）本身含「不」的意思，**不能再和 not 並用**；**部分否定**（not + all／both／every／always → 「並非全部」）≠ **全部否定**（none／neither／not any）；**雙重否定**負負得正（cannot…without、not unusual）；還有**不用否定字的隱性否定**（the last…to、anything but、far from、fail to…）與一票**否定慣用表現**（by no means、no longer、cannot…too、not…until）。

## 📊 否定的五種面向

| 面向 | 關鍵 | 例 |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **否定字** | not／no／never／hardly／seldom／few／little（不與 not 並用） | It **never** snows here. |
| **部分否定** | not + all／both／every／always → 並非全部 | She does **not always** agree with me. |
| **全部否定** | none／neither／not any → 一個也不 | **None** of them went abroad. |
| **雙重否定** | 兩個否定 → 肯定（負負得正） | You **can't** make an omelet **without** breaking eggs. |
| **隱性否定** | 無否定字、意義卻是否定 | He is the **last** person **to** tell a lie. |

## 📗 否定字（本身含「不」，不能與 not 並用）

### not（不）
- 本動詞否定：`助動詞 + not + 原形`：My boss did **not** tell me to wear jeans.
- 不定詞否定：`not + to + 原形`（不需助動詞）：My boss told me **not to** wear jeans.
- **對比用 not**（省略重複主詞動詞）：Dave is an Australian, **not** an Austrian.／I met him on the street, **not** at the office.
- **not because… but because…**（不是因為…而是因為…）：She loves him **not because** he is handsome, **but because** he is warmhearted.

### no（沒有、零）
- `not + a/an + 單數 = no + 單數`；`not any = no`。
- no 後接單數／不可數 → 單數動詞；接複數 → 複數動詞：**No** students **are** allowed to enter.／**Nobody is** allowed to enter.
- **加強語氣**：He is **no** genius.（＝絕非天才，比 not a genius 強）。
- I have **no one to count on.** = I **do not** have anyone to count on.（not any → no，助動詞 do 也隨之消失）。

### never（絕不、從不，頻率副詞）
> 位置：**be 動詞／助動詞之後、一般動詞之前**。

- It **never snows** in the Sahara Desert.（永恆真理用現在式）
- You should **never** walk alone at night.（加強語氣）

### hardly／scarcely／rarely／seldom（幾乎不／很少）
> 都含「不」→ 否定字；位置同 never（be／助動詞後、一般動詞前）。

- His success is **hardly** possible.（幾乎不 → 後面配 possible，不用 impossible）
- The injured child could **scarcely** walk.／I **rarely** listen to classical music.
- England has **seldom** won the World Cup**, has it?**（否定字 → 附加問句用**肯定**）

### few／little（很少、幾乎沒有）
> 「幾乎沒有」是**說話者比例概念**上的問題（實際還是有一點）。`few + 可數複數`（= not many）／`little + 不可數`（= not much）。

- **Few** (people) **are** satisfied with his answer.（還是有 → 複數動詞）
- He showed **little** interest in math.

## 🟠 部分否定（並非全部）
> **not + 表「全部／總是」的字**（all／both／every／always／necessarily…）→ 一律解作**「並非…」**。（英文「全部」指數量 3 以上；both 指 2）

- **Both** of my friends did **not** go abroad.（並非兩位都出國 → 只有一位去）
- He did **not** read **all** of these comic books.（並非全部都讀）
- She does **not always** agree with me.（並非總是 = sometimes she doesn't）
- **Not everybody** can be a poet.
- Beautiful flowers do **not necessarily** smell sweet.（未必都香）
- ⚠️ **quite／altogether／completely／entirely／exactly + not** 也構成部分否定。

---

## 🔴 全部否定（一個也不）
> 對照部分否定，用 **none／neither／not any**。

- Both…not（部分）↔ **Neither**／**None** of my friends went abroad.（全部否定）
- not all（部分）↔ He did **not** read **any** of these comic books. = He read **none** of them.
- ⚠️ **neither／none 本身是否定字**，不可再和 not 並用。

---

## ➕➕ 雙重否定（負負得正）
> 兩個否定 → 肯定，語氣更強。**否定字（never／none／neither／seldom…）不可用於雙重否定**（不能和 not 並用）。

- You **can't** make an omelet **without** breaking some eggs.（要做蛋捲就得打蛋；without 為介系詞 → 後接 V-ing）
- It is **not unusual** for couples to quarrel.（並非不尋常 → 很正常；比 usual 語氣強）

---

## 🕵️ 不用否定字的否定表現（隱性否定）
> 整句沒有否定字，意義卻是否定。

| 表現 | 意義 | 例 |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Who knows…? | 沒人知道 | **Who knows** what will happen tomorrow?（= No one knows） |
| too … to … | 太…而不能… | This book was **too** boring for me **to** read through. |
| the last … to V | 最不可能…（絕不會） | He would be the **last** person **to** tell a lie. |
| anything but | 一點也不 | The story he told me was **anything but** boring. |
| far from | 絕不、遠非 | Five hundred dollars… is **far from** cheap! |
| free from | 免於、沒有 | This district is **free from** air pollution. |
| beyond（description…） | 在…之外 → 難以… | beautiful **beyond description**（美得難以形容） |
| above | 跳脫…之上 → 不屑／不會 | This book is quite **above** me.（超過我理解）／He is **above** cheating. |
| fail to V | 未能 | The alarm clock **failed to** go off. |
| keep／stop／prevent… from | 阻止、使無法 | Heavy fog **kept** us **from** driving fast. |
| refrain from | 避免 | You had better **refrain from** alcohol. |
| know better than to V | 不至於笨到去… | I **know better than to** do such a thing. |
| in vain | 枉然、徒勞 | We tried to open the door, but **in vain.** |

> **阻止／防止**同義群：keep／hinder／stop／protect／prevent … **from** …

## 🧷 否定的慣用表現

| 句型 | 意義 | 例 |
| --- | --- | --- |
| by no means | 絕不、絕非 | He is **by no means** a fool.（= no fool = not a fool at all） |
| no longer（= not … any longer） | 不再 | Traveling to the moon is **no longer** a dream. |
| cannot … too … | 再…也不為過 | You **cannot be too** careful when you drive at night.（負負得正） |
| it is not long before… | 不久後就… | It was **not long before** the earthquake stopped. |
| not … until … | 直到…才… | I **didn't** graduate **until** I was twenty-five. |
| It is not until … that …（強調） | 直到…才…（強調句） | **It was not until** I was 25 **that** I graduated. |
| do nothing but + 原形 | 只會… | Babies **do nothing but** cry. |
| cannot help + V-ing（= cannot but + 原形） | 不得不、忍不住 | I **cannot help** admiring his courage. |
| There is no + V-ing | …是不可能的 | **There is no telling** what may happen. |
| not to mention（= to say nothing of） | 更不用說 | He can ride a motorcycle, **not to mention** a bicycle. |
| lost no time in + V-ing | 立刻去做 | The soldier **lost no time in** carrying it out. |

> `not to mention` 前面須為完整句子＋逗號；**let alone／much less** 用於前面為**否定**的句子。

## ⚠️ 易錯點分析

> [!WARNING]
> **常見錯誤（皆為來源整理的重點）**
> - **否定字（no／never／hardly／seldom／neither／none）不能再和 not 並用**；否定字句的**附加問句用肯定**（…seldom…, has it?）。
> - **部分否定 ≠ 全部否定**：not + all／both／every／always＝「並非全部」；全部否定用 none／neither／not any。
> - **hardly／scarcely 已含「不」**，後面別再放否定字（hardly **possible**，非 impossible）。
> - **few／little＝幾乎沒有**（實際仍有一點，few 接複數動詞）；a few／a little 才是肯定「有一些」。
> - **雙重否定負負得正**：cannot…without、not unusual、cannot…too；隱性否定 the last…to、anything but、far from、fail to、above、in vain 等要能一眼認出。
> - **not…until**：到該時間點前都沒發生；強調句型 **It is not until … that …**（只能用 that 子句）。

- [ ] Q2：改錯：England has seldom won the World Cup, **hasn't** it?
- [ ] Q3：hardly 還是 impossible：His success is ___ possible.
- [ ] Q4：雙重否定改寫（正面語氣）：You can't succeed without working hard.
- [ ] Q5：用 not…until 與其強調句改寫：He didn't call me until midnight.
- [ ] Q6：判斷是否否定：He is the last person to betray a friend.

<details>
<summary>✅ 解答</summary>

A1：並非兩位都 → **Both** of them did **not** go abroad（部分否定）；兩位都沒 → **Neither**／**None** of them went abroad（全部否定，用否定字、不加 not）。
A2：seldom 是否定字（否定）→ 附加問句用**肯定** → **has it?**
A3：hardly 已含「不」→ 後接 **possible**（His success is hardly possible）。
A4：You **can succeed only by** working hard.／要做…就得努力（負負得正＝必須努力才能成功）。
A5：He **didn't** call me **until** midnight. → 強調：**It was not until** midnight **that** he called me.
A6：**是否定**——the last…to＝「絕不會」→ 他絕不會背叛朋友。
