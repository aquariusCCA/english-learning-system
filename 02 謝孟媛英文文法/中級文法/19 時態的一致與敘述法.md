# 時態的一致與敘述法

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **一句話核心**
> **時態的一致**：主要子句是**過去式**時，從屬（that）子句必須用**過去時態**（過去式／過去完成式／過去式助動詞 would/could…）——「過去的人講的未來仍在過去，與現在無關」。**敘述法**：把別人的話原句加引號＝**直接敘述**；轉述、改成 that 子句＝**間接敘述**（要改**動詞時態、人稱代名詞、時間地方副詞**三樣）。

## 📐 時態的一致（依主要子句動詞）
| 主要子句動詞 | 從屬（that）子句可用 |
| --- | --- |
| **現在式** | 任何時態（依句意）：My sister **knows** that I **love**／**loved**／**will fall** in love… |
| **現在完成式** | 現在式／過去式／現在完成式：I **have heard** that he **works**／**worked**／**has worked** hard. |
| **過去式** | 過去式／過去完成式／**過去式助動詞 would/could**：My sister **knew** that I **loved**／**had loved**／**would fall**… |
| **過去完成式** | 只能過去完成式（時間線最左、限制最多）：I **had wondered** why there **had been**… |
| **未來式** | 現在/過去/現在完成/未來式：He **will say** that he **is**／**was**／**has been**／**will be** fine. |

> 💬 **AI 補充**（整合第三方整理文，把章節中以截圖呈現的規則轉為文字）：過去式主句下，would＝過去裡的未來（≠ will）、had p.p.＝過去裡更早的事。「越往時間線左端，限制越多。」

---

## ⚠️ 時態一致的例外（從屬子句照本意，不隨主句後移）
| 情形 | 用 | 例 |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **不變的真理／諺語** | 現在式 | The child learned that the earth **goes** round the sun.／The teacher told us that practice **makes** perfect. |
| **至今未變的習慣／事實** | 現在式 | She often told me that she **keeps** early hours.／She told me that school **begins** at eight. |
| **歷史的事實** | 過去式 | The teacher said that Columbus **discovered** America in 1492.／He said that World War II **ended** in 1945. |
| **假設語氣** | 不受限（照假設語氣規則） | He said that if he **were** a bird he **could** fly.／He spoke English as if he **were** a native speaker. |

> ⚠️ 三個時態都分布時，**以現在式為主**（習慣/事實延續至今）。

## 🗣️ 敘述法：直接 vs 間接

### 基本轉換
| 句型 | 直接敘述 | 間接敘述 |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 無 to 人 | S + say, "…" | S + say **(that)** + 子句 |
| 有 to 人 | S + say **to** 人, "…" | S + **tell** 人 **(that)** + 子句 |

- Bill says, "**I** hate math." → Bill says (that) **he hates** math.（主詞 I→he、動詞加 s）
- ⚠️ **say（對…說）→ 間接改 tell（告訴…）**；that 子句要**符合時態一致**。

### 間接敘述要改三樣
**① 動詞時態**（主句過去 → 子句後移）
- May said, "I **like**…" → May said she **liked**…
- Helen answered, "The rain **has stopped**." → …the rain **had stopped**.（比過去更早 → 過去完成）
- She said, "I **read** this book." → she **had read** that book.

**② 人稱代名詞／所有格**（隨轉述者立場改）
- She says, "I don't like **my** hairstyle." → she doesn't like **her** hairstyle.
- I said to her, "**I'm** sorry **you** don't understand **me**." → I told her (that) **I was** sorry **she** didn't understand **me**.

**③ 時間、地方副詞**
> 💬 **AI 補充**（章節此表為截圖，補上第三方對照文的文字版）：

| 直接 | 間接 |
| --- | --- |
| now | then／at once（若「現在」義可直接刪去） |
| today | that day |
| yesterday | the day before／the previous day |
| tomorrow | the next day／the following day |
| last week/year | the previous week/year |
| next week/year | the following week/year |
| ~ ago | ~ before |
| here | there |
| this／these | that／those |

> ⚠️ 副詞改法**非絕對**：若直接與間接敘述在**同一天**，日期副詞不變；相隔一兩天則依實際調整（the day after tomorrow → tomorrow／today）。

## 🔀 各種句型的間接敘述

### 直述句（肯定／否定）
> `S + say(said) (that) + 直述句`；有 to 人改 `tell 人`。
- She said, "I'**m going to** quit my job." → She said she **was going to** quit her job.
- She said to me, "I **can** go… **tomorrow**." → She told me she **could** go… **the next day**.

### 疑問句 → 動詞改 ask
| 類型 | 間接句型 |
| --- | --- |
| **Yes/No** | S + **ask (人) + if／whether** + 主詞 + 動詞 |
| **WH** | S + **ask (人) + 疑問詞** + 主詞 + 動詞（不再倒裝） |

- Tom said to her, "How **can I** get to your house from **here**?" → Tom asked her **how he could** get to her house from **there**.
- He said to me, "**Have** you ever **seen** a panda?" → He asked me **if I had seen** a panda.（經驗 → 過去完成）

### 祈使句 → `tell/ask/order… 人 (not) to V`
> 依語氣選動詞：命令 tell/order/command；要求 ask/beg/request；勸告 advise；提議 suggest/propose；禁止 forbid/prohibit。

- Mother said to me, "**Come** back by five." → Mother **told me to come** back by five.（兇→ ordered）
- My father said, "**Don't** give up **your** hope." → My father **told me not to** give up **my** hope.（never 語氣更強）
- **suggest/order/request 後接 that 子句**：that 不可省、**should 可省（省後仍用原形）**：She said, "Let's go for a walk." → She **suggested that we (should) go** for a walk.
- **禁止**：The teacher said, "Don't leave your seats." → The teacher **forbade us to leave**… ／ **prohibited us from leaving**…（forbid + 人 + to V／prohibit + 人 + from V-ing）

### 感嘆句 → 動詞改 exclaim/cry/shout…，可加 very/really
- Ms. Lee said, "**What a good idea** you have!" → Ms. Lee said (that) I had **a very good** idea.／Ms. Lee **exclaimed** (that)…
- 祈願句類似（用 pray/express + wish）：He said, "May God help us!" → He **prayed** that God **might** help them.

### 合句（and/but/or 連兩對等子句）
- 各子句前補 that，**第一個 that 可省、第二個不可省**：He said, "She is out, but she will be back soon." → He said (that) she **was** out **but that** she **would** be back soon.
- **連接詞為 for／as（因為）→ 保留、不改 that**：Mom said, "Dad doesn't know it, as he **has not heard**…" → …as he **had not heard**…
- **祈使句 + and／or → 保留 and/or**（表結果/警惕），也可改 if／unless：He said to me, "Get up at once, **or** you will be late." → He told me to get up at once, **or** I **would** be late.（= …**unless** I got up…／**if** I got up…, I would not be late.）

### 複句（從屬連接詞連兩子句）
> 只須遵守基本規則。
- She said to me, "I **gave** him chocolates **because** I **like** him." → She told me she **had given** him chocolates because she **liked** him.（給＝過去完成；喜歡＝事實→過去式）

## ⚠️ 易錯點分析

> [!WARNING]
> **常見錯誤（皆為來源整理的重點）**
> - **主句過去 → that 子句用過去時態**；would＝過去裡的未來（不用 will）、had p.p.＝過去裡更早。
> - **例外**：真理/習慣/事實 → 現在式；歷史事實 → 過去式；假設語氣不受限。
> - **say（to 人）→ tell 人**；that 子句符合時態一致。
> - 間接敘述改**三樣**：時態、人稱代名詞/所有格、時間地方副詞（now→then、here→there、this→that、ago→before）。
> - **疑問句 → ask**（Yes/No 用 if/whether、WH 用疑問詞，皆**不倒裝**）。
> - **祈使句 → (not) to V**；命令/要求/勸告/禁止選對動詞；**suggest 等 that 不可省、should 可省**。
> - **合句**：第一個 that 可省、第二個不可省；**for/as、and/or 保留不改 that**。
> - 副詞改法**非絕對**，看實際談話時間微調。

A3：She said (that) she **liked** pop music.
A4：I asked him **if/whether he was** going abroad.
A5：Father **told me not to** give up.
A6：Tom asked **how he could** get **there** from **there**.（here→there）
