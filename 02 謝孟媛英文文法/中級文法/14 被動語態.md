# 被動語態

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **一句話核心**
> 被動語態 = **be 動詞 + 過去分詞**（**be 管時態、p.p. 管被動**）。**只有及物動詞（含及物的動詞片語）才有被動**（第三、四、五句型；第一、二句型不及物→無被動）。轉換：**受詞 ↔ 主詞、動詞 → be + p.p.、原主詞 → by + 行為者**（行為者泛指／不明／可由前後文推知時，**by 可省**）。

## 📊 各時態的被動形式
| 時態 | 被動語態（The boy … punished） |
| --- | --- |
| 現在式 | is punished |
| 過去式 | was punished |
| 未來式 | **will be** punished |
| 現在進行 | is **being** punished |
| 過去進行 | was **being** punished |
| 現在完成 | has **been** punished |
| 過去完成 | had **been** punished |
| 未來完成 | will **have been** punished |

> ⚠️ 未來式別寫 will + p.p.（will 是助動詞，後接原形 → **will be** p.p.）。

---

## 🔄 主被動轉換
> 步驟：主詞①、動詞②、受詞③ → **③換到主詞位、②改 be + p.p.、①移後加 by**（by＝被，接**行為者**）。時間/地方副詞位置不變。

- People all over the world watch the Olympics. → The Olympics **is watched by** people all over the world.
- 含 be 動詞的句子否定：be + **not** + p.p.（Talking on cell phones **is not permitted**…）。

### by + 行為者可省略的三種情形
| 情形 | 例 |
| --- | --- |
| 動作者為一般人／泛指一群人 | Wild monkeys **are seen** around this area. |
| 動作者不清楚 | This seat **is taken**. |
| 前後文可知動作者 | The newspapers **are delivered** around 5 a.m. |

> **動詞片語（= 及物動詞）也可被動**：The wind had blown away the balloons → The balloons **had been blown away** by the wind.

## 🧩 各句型的被動

### 第四句型 S+V+O+O（授與動詞，兩受詞取一當主詞）
- 人當主詞：They sent all the customers a card. → All the customers **were sent** a card.
- 物當主詞：A card **was sent to** all the customers.（物在前、人在後要介系詞）
- ⚠️ **被動中授與動詞的介系詞是 to 常省略**（was given (to) me）；on／for／in 等**不可省**。
- ⚠️ **buy／get／make／read／sing／write／build／cook／pass／choose** 的被動**多不用間接受詞（人）當主詞**：A motorcycle was bought **for** me（不用 I was bought…）。

### 第五句型 S+V+O+C（補語留下補受詞）
- The baby **was named** Doris.／The wall **was painted** blue.／I **was persuaded to go** to the dentist.
- **使役動詞被動**：
  | 使役動詞 | 主動 | 被動 |
  | --- | --- | --- |
  | make、have | O + V（原形） | O + **to V**（The students were made **to wait**.） |
  | let | O + V | O + **be p.p.** |
  | get | O + to V | O + **p.p.** |
- **感官動詞被動**：主動的原形不定詞 → 被動改 **to V**（或 V-ing）：His dog **was heard to bark**／**barking**.／A man **was seen to jump**／**jumping** into the river.

### 疑問句的被動
- **Yes/No**：`be 動詞 + 主詞 + p.p.…?`（Are your meals **cooked** by your wife?／Will the work **be finished** tomorrow?）
- **WH**：疑問詞放句首 + 被動：Who **is suspected** by the police?／Who(m) **was** this CD **produced by**?（= By whom was this CD produced?）

### 祈使句的被動
- 加 **Let**：`Let + 受詞 + be + p.p.`（Open the door → **Let** the door **be opened**.）
- 否定：**Let** this lesson **not be forgotten.** = **Don't let** this lesson be forgotten.
- 本就有 let 者保留：Let my daughters pack… → **Let** the things **be packed** by my daughters.

### Say／believe／think／expect／know／consider 類（受詞為 that 子句）
> 兩種被動：`It is + p.p. + that + S + V`（假主詞 it）／`S is + p.p. + to V`。

- They say that yawns are catching. → **It is said that** yawns are catching. = Yawns **are said to be** catching.
- We expect that the plane will land on time. → **It is expected that**… = The plane **is expected to land** on time.

## ⚠️ 特別注意的被動語態

### 行為者前不加 by
- **情緒動詞的被動**（人的情緒被外在引起，介系詞多變、非 by）：be **surprised at**、be **shocked at**、be **satisfied with**、be **absorbed in**、be **interested in**。
- **「被…所知」**：be **known to**（為…所知）／be **known as**（以…身分聞名）。（對比：The language is **known by** everyone → 每人主動去認識，用 by。）

### 過去分詞修飾名詞（= 被動形容詞）
- home-made cakes、**excited** spectators（人的情緒 → 被動 → p.p.）。

### 不定詞、動名詞的被動
- to V → **to be p.p.**（I don't like **to be talked about**.）／V-ing → **being p.p.**（Nobody likes **being made fun of**.）

### 被動表「動作」vs「狀態」
- 動作：The street lights **are switched on** at sunset.（某人打開）
- 狀態：The street lights **are switched**（= 亮著的）。
- **強調動作用 become／get 代替 be**（She **got dressed** quickly.）；**強調狀態用 lie／stand／remain／stay**（The door **remained closed**.／The city **lies buried**.）

### 主動語態表被動（強調本質，非特定動作者；限部分動詞）
- Chicken doesn't **bake** well.／Your paper **reads** like a novel.／Red wine stains don't **wash** out easily.／The door doesn't **lock**.／This steak doesn't **cut** easily.

### 經驗性被動：have／get + O + p.p.（被害）
- I **had** my house **burned down**.／I **got** my pocket **picked**.／I **got** my wrist **broken**.
- ⚠️ 亦可純被動（非被害），看前後文：I **had my hair cut.**（純被動，剪髮）。

### 慣用的被動語態
| 慣用 | 意義／要點 |
| --- | --- |
| be born | 出生（bear-bore-**born**）；生產 = bear-bore-**borne**（主動：My mom has **borne** four children.） |
| be married | 結婚（狀態）；持續多久用 for（have been married seven years） |
| be injured／wounded／hurt | 受傷（injure 泛指／wound 刀槍／hurt 肉體或精神） |
| be acquainted with | 熟悉 |
| be engaged in／to | 從事（in）／訂婚（to someone） |
| be used／accustomed to + N/V-ing | 習慣（to 是**介系詞**，接名詞/動名詞；≠ used to V 過去習慣） |
| be seated | 坐下（= sit down = seat oneself） |

## ⚠️ 易錯點分析

> [!WARNING]
> **常見錯誤（皆為來源整理的重點）**
> - **be + p.p.**（be 管時態、p.p. 管被動）；**未來 will be p.p.**、完成 have been p.p.。
> - **只有及物動詞（含及物片語）有被動**；1、2 句型無被動。
> - **by 可省**（泛指／不明／可推知）；**情緒動詞被動不用 by、介系詞多變**（surprised at、satisfied with）。
> - 第四句型：**to 常省、on/for 不可省**；buy 類**不用人當主詞**。
> - 第五句型使役：**make/have → to V、let → be p.p.、get → p.p.**；感官被動用 to V/V-ing。
> - **say/think 類**：It is said that…／S is said to V。
> - **動作 vs 狀態**（become/get 強調動作、lie/remain 強調狀態）；部分動詞**主動表被動**（doesn't lock）。
> - **have/get + O + p.p.**＝被害（或純被動）；**be born（出生）vs bear-bore-borne（生產）**。

<summary>✅ 解答</summary>

A4：**It is said that** he is honest. = He **is said to be** honest.
A5：had my hair cut＝純被動（剪髮）；had my money stolen＝被害。
