# 不定詞

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **一句話核心**
> 不定詞 = **to + 原形動詞**，身分不固定（不再是動詞），在句中當 **名詞**（主詞／受詞／補語）、**形容詞**（後位修飾名詞）、**副詞**（目的／原因／結果／條件…）。另有 **原形不定詞**（不加 to，用於**感官／使役動詞**後）。不定詞可再變化為 **被動 `to be p.p.`**、**進行 `to be V-ing`**、**完成 `to have p.p.`**。否定一律 **`not／never + to V`**（不需助動詞）。

## 📊 三種用法總覽

| 用法 | 功能 | 例 |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **名詞用法** | 當主詞、受詞、補語 | **To please** everyone is impossible. |
| **形容詞用法** | **後位**修飾名詞／代名詞 | Give me something **to eat**. |
| **副詞用法** | 修飾動詞／形容詞／副詞，表目的、原因、結果等 | He went there **to eat** pizza. |

> `to + 原形動詞` 為一般不定詞；**原形不定詞**長得跟原形動詞一樣（去掉 to），用於感官／使役動詞之後。身分之所以會變，是因為**它在句中的位置變了**。

## 📗 名詞用法（主詞／補語／受詞）

### 當主詞
> [!WARNING]
> **不定詞放句首當主詞時視為「一件事」，其後接單數動詞。**

- **To please** everyone at the same time **is** impossible.（在同一時間取悅每個人是不可能的。）
- **To protect** the environment **is** important.
- **假主詞 it**：不定詞當主詞太長 → 用 it 代替、事件放後面，避免頭重腳輕：
  - **It is** impossible **to please** everyone at the same time.
  - **It is** important **to protect** the environment.

**意義上的主詞（對某人而言）用 for；表人格特質用 of：**
- `It's + 形容詞(修飾事物) + for + 人 + to V`（for 是介系詞，後接受格）
  - It is impossible **for me** to please everyone at the same time.
  - It is difficult **for me** to answer this question.
- `It's + 形容詞(表人格特質) + of + 人 + to V`
  - 修飾「人」的形容詞：good、nice、kind、brave、clever、careless、honest、bad、stupid、silly、selfish、polite…
  - It's very kind **of** you to see me off. = **You're** very kind to see me off.
  - It was stupid **of** me to do such a thing. = **I was** stupid to do such a thing.

> [!TIP]
> **for／of 由前面的形容詞決定。** of 句型（修飾人）可改成用「人」當主詞；for 句型（修飾事物）則不行。
> - see（人）off 是**可分**動詞片語：see my uncle off／see off my uncle／see him off。

### 當補語
> be 動詞、連綴動詞後接**補語**（左右相等）；一般動詞後接**受詞**。不定詞具名詞特性，可當補語。

- A doctor's duty is **to take care of** sick people.（醫生的職責是照顧病人。sick people = the sick）
- His fault is **to talk too much.**

### 當受詞
- We decided **to put off** the meeting.（decide 後接動詞只能用 to V；把 to put off the meeting 換成 it → We decided **it** → 故為受詞）
- My son needs **to see** a dentist.
  - **need to V**：需要去做某事，主詞多為**人**（主動）。
  - **need V-ing**：某事需要**被**完成（被動語意，≒ need to be p.p.）。
  - 💬 **三種說法可互換**（補充自 english.cool〈need〉）：Her room **needs cleaning.** = Her room **needs to be cleaned.** = She **needs to clean** her room.

### 否定形 `not + to V`
- **Not to depend on** others is the best way to success.（不依賴別人是成功的最佳途徑。）
- 為什麼不需助動詞？加了助動詞就得寫「助動詞 + not + 原形」，to 得去掉 → 一句裡出現兩個動詞。故不定詞否定**直接在前面加 not**。

### 疑問詞 + to V（名詞片語，可當主／受／補）
> 名詞子句若①主詞能由上下文推知（與主要子句同主詞、或雙方都知道指誰）②含助動詞 can／could／should，即可簡化成「**疑問詞 + to V**」。常用疑問詞：what、when、where、whether、how、whom、which。

- 當受詞：The clever boy knew **what to do** to solve the problem.（← what he could do）
- 當主詞：**How to reduce** the costs is a big problem.
- 當補語：The problem is **where to set up** the tent.
- 當受詞：I didn't know **whether to laugh or** (to) get angry.（or 為對等連接詞，主詞動詞重複可省）

> ⚠️ **疑問詞後碰到動詞一定用不定詞**（主詞省略、助動詞一併省略）。

## 📘 形容詞用法（後位修飾）

> 一個字的形容詞放名詞**前**，兩字以上放名詞**後**。不定詞至少兩個詞 → 一律**後位修飾**，形成 `名詞 + to V`。

### 修飾名詞
- Amy was the only person **to remember** my birthday.
- I didn't have a chance **to use English** in Japan.（a chance to speak English 亦可）

### 「(代)名詞 + to V + 介系詞」：不定詞前的(代)名詞為介系詞的受詞
- I have parents **to take care of.**（← take care of parents；受詞跑到前面，**of 不可省**）
- I want a knife **to cut it with.**（← cut it with a knife）

> ⚠️ 動詞片語的介系詞別漏掉：有受詞才用 of（take care **of**），無受詞則省（take care＝小心）。

### 修飾名詞的不定詞可用關係代名詞代換
> 關係代名詞引導的是形容詞子句，故可互換。

- I'm looking for someone **to help me.** = …someone **who will help** me.
- Do you have anything **to write with?** = …anything **which** I/you **can write with?**（← write with a pen）

## 📙 副詞用法（修飾動詞／形容詞／副詞）

> 可表**時間、地點、條件、理由、目的、原因、方法**等。

### 表目的（為了）
- 可換成 **in order to** / **so as to**（僅表目的的不定詞可這樣換）。
- He went to Germany **to study** music. = …**in order to** study／**so as to** study music.
- The window was open a few inches **to keep** the air circulating.

### 表原因（跟在表**感情**的形容詞後）
- I was shocked **to see** the scene.
- We could not but laugh **to see** it.（cannot but + 原形＝不禁、忍不住）

### 表結果（有時伴隨 only、never，含「失望」意味）
- He awoke in the middle of the night **to see** him softly go out of the room.（無失望 → 不加 only／never）
- We were separated never **to see** each other again.
- The police hurried to the house only **to find (that)** it was empty.（結果只發現空無一人）

### 表條件（修飾假設語氣主要子句，代替 if 子句）
> 與現在事實相反用過去式、與過去事實相反用過去完成式；**事實與假設差一級**。

- **To hear** him talk on the phone, you would take him for his brother. = **If you heard**…（take + 人 + for + 另一人＝把某人視為…）
- I would give you anything **to go** to the concert. = …**if I could go**…

### 表「難、易」：形容詞 + to V
- Poetry is hard **to translate.**（Poetry 視為總集合 → 單數）
- This rule is easy **to remember.**

### 「be + 形容詞 + to V」慣用表現
| 片語 | 意義 | 例 |
| --- | --- | --- |
| be apt to | 易於… | We are apt **to make** mistakes. |
| be ready to | 願意、易於… | I am ready **to help** you. |
| be willing to | 樂意…（= be glad to） | I am willing **to come** with you. |
| be eager to | 渴望… | He is eager **to see** you. |
| be likely to | 可能… | It is likely **to rain** soon.（= It looks like rain） |
| be anxious to | 急著想要… | He is anxious **to go** abroad. |

### 程度句型（可與 so … that 代換）
> **形容詞配 be 動詞、副詞配一般動詞；形容詞／副詞放在 enough 之前。**

- **… enough to …**（夠～可以～）：She was kind **enough to offer** me her seat. = She was **so kind that** she offered…
  - enough 當形容詞時名詞可放前或後（enough money／money enough）；enough ↔ sufficient(ly)。
- **too … to …**（太～而不能～，本身已含否定）：
  - This bed is too small for me **to sleep in.** = so small **that** I cannot sleep in.（改 so…that 就不需要 for me）
  - I am **only too glad to** help you.（only too + happy／glad／pleased = very）
- **not too … to** ／ **too … not to**（雙重否定 → 肯定）：
  - He is **not too proud to** ask questions of others.（不至於驕傲得不能問人 → 願意問）
  - He is **too wise not to** see the reason.（聰明到不可能不懂 → 一定懂）= so wise that he cannot but see…
- **so … as to V**（非常～以致於…）= so … that：
  - He was **so angry as to** be unable to speak. = so angry **that** he was unable to speak.
  - I am **not so foolish as to** trust him. = not **such a fool as to** trust him.（so + 形容詞／副詞；such + a/an + 單數名詞）

## 🧩 不定詞當受詞補語的句型

### S + V + O + **to V**（受詞後一定用不定詞當補語）
| 類別 | 動詞 | 例 |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 表希望、期待 | like、want、wish、expect | I want you **to tell** the truth.／I didn't expect her **to be** absent. |
| 表許可 | allow、permit | Please allow me **to introduce** my wife.（permit 較正式；口語用 Allow me） |
| 表思考、認識 | think、believe、consider、find、recognize | We believe him **to be** honest. = We believe (that) he is honest. |
| 表依賴、建議、要求、命令 | tell、advise、order、ask、beg、request、require、force、persuade、get、cause、teach | The doctor advised him **to stop** smoking.／I persuaded him **to go** with me. |

### S + V + O + **原形不定詞**（感官／使役動詞後）
> **原形不定詞**＝不加 to 的不定詞。感官動詞與使役動詞是「兄弟」，後面都用原形不定詞。

- **感官動詞**（感覺 + 五官）：feel、see、watch、look at、notice、observe、hear、listen to
  - I felt my heart **beat** violently.／I saw the boy **put** a chocolate in his pocket.
  - ⚠️ **被動語態要還原成 to 不定詞**：The police officer watched the children **cross** → The children were watched **to cross** the road.
- **使役動詞**：make（迫使）、have（依賴、拜託）、let（許可）、bid（命令）
  - The mother made the boy **turn off** the TV.／The teacher let the pupils **go** home.
  - **get** 為使役時用 **to V**：I got him **to repair** the radio.
  - **help** 後接 **(to) V** 皆可：Can you help me **(to) put up** the tent?
  - ⚠️ 使役動詞中**只有 make 的被動要用 to 不定詞**（make 有強迫意味）：The teacher made the students try again → The students were made **to try** again.

### S + V + **it** + C + to V（假受詞 it）
> 動詞多為 find、think、make、consider…；it 是**假受詞**代替一件事，真正內容用後面不定詞交代。

- She found **it** impossible **to understand** him.（= found **that** it is impossible…；it 變受詞則 that 消失）
- I think **it** wrong **to tell** a lie.（事情才有對錯）
- I think **it** careless **of** you **to lose** your watch.（意義主詞 for 指事、of 指人）
- He makes **it** a rule **to keep** good hours.（make it a rule to…＝習慣做…）

## 🔎 接不定詞的特殊動詞：seem／appear／happen／prove／come to V

> 這些動詞後常接不定詞；多可用 `It + 動詞 + that + S + V` 代換（**有多一個主詞用 that，否則用 to V**）。

| 動詞 | 意義 | 例 |
| --- | --- | --- |
| seem／appear + to V | 似乎～ | My dog **seems to understand** what I say. = It seems that my dog understands… |
| happen／chance + to V | 碰巧～（主詞為**人**） | I **happened to meet** one of my friends at the station. |
| prove／turn out + to V | 結果是～、證明是～ | The rumor **proved (turned out) to be** true. |
| come／get + to V | 變得～ | You will **come to like** this town. |

- seem／appear 後也可直接接形容詞：The children **appeared (to be) pleased**（to be 可省）。
- ⚠️ **prove／turn out 只接不定詞**，與 seem／appear 不同；且 It 假主詞句型只有 turn out 有（❌ It proved that…）。
- happen 解「發生」時主詞是**事情**（事情 + happen to + 人）；解「碰巧」時主詞是**人**（人 + happen + to V）。

## ⏳ be + 不定詞

| 用法 | 意義 | 例 |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 表**預定** | ≒ will／be going to（be 多用現在式） | The meeting **is to be held** on Wednesday. |
| 表**義務** | 必須 | Rules **are to be observed.**／You **are to start** as early as possible. |
| 表**可能** | ≒ can（否定句常用被動） | Not a cloud **was to be seen** in the sky.／Nobody **was to be found** on the street. |
| 表**命運** | 註定（「命運」字面常不出現） | He **was never to see** his wife and family again. |
| 表**意圖** | 想要、打算（= intend；常用 if 子句） | If **you are to succeed**, you must work harder. |

## 🔄 不定詞的三態變化

| 變化 | 形式 | 例 |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 被動 | **to be p.p.** | I like **to be taught.**／There is a lot **to be done** yet. |
| 進行 | **to be V-ing** | They seem **to be waiting** for us. |
| 完成 | **to have p.p.** | The child seems **to have been** sick. |

### 完成式不定詞 `to have p.p.`：表示比述語動詞（本動詞）**更早**的時間
- The child seems **to be** sick.（現在的狀態）→ The child seems **to have been** sick.（早就生病＝過去式／現在完成式的時間）
- He seemed **to have spent** the money. = It seemed that he **had spent** the money.（seemed 過去式 → 更早用過去完成式）
- 為何不直接用過去式？因為 to 後面要接原形；為區別「以前」與「現在」，用 `to have + p.p.` 表更早的時間。

### 完成式不定詞的延伸　💬 AI 補充
> 補充自 Facebook〈完成式不定詞〉與〈不定詞完成型結構〉兩篇；忠實整理其重點，非謝孟媛講義。

- **表過去事實**（常接 claim、seem、pretend、be said、be thought…）：Peter **claims to have met** the president. = claims (that) he **has met**…／I seem **to have lost** my way.
- **表與過去事實相反（未實現）**：
  - `be to have p.p.`＝過去計畫好卻沒發生（= was／were supposed to；be 只能用 was／were）：Jack **was to have gone** on holiday…（沒去成）。
  - `would like／love／prefer to have p.p.` ／ `wanted to have p.p.`＝過去未實現的願望：My brother **would like to have studied** abroad.（沒出國）= would have liked to study abroad.
  - `pretend to have p.p.`：She **pretended to have** just broken up with her boyfriend.
- **表比主動詞更晚（未來完成）**：I hope **to have got** a doctor's degree by the end of July.
- **完成式不定詞當主詞**：**To have got** a doctor's degree from MIT **was** a great achievement.（已取得）／…**would have been** a great achievement, but that was just a dream.（未取得）

### 助動詞 + have + p.p.（**對過去做推測／未實現的事**）
> 這裡是**原形不定詞**（to 省略）；助動詞後接原形，故為 `助動詞 + have + p.p.`。兩事有先後時，先發生的用完成式。

- **can't／couldn't + have p.p.**：對過去的**否定推測**
  - She **can't have left** yet.／He **couldn't have forgotten** my birthday.／They **couldn't have been** on strike.（罷工為持續狀態 → 完成式）
- **must + have p.p.**：對過去的**肯定推測**
  - He **must have studied** hard to pass the exam.／Her mom **must have been** seriously ill then.
- **should／ought to + have p.p.**：**未能實現**的事（帶責備）
  - You **should have seen** it.（弦外之音：你沒看）／You **ought to have kept** your word.（你沒遵守）
- **intend／wish／would like／expect + to have p.p.**：**沒有實現**的事
  - I **intended to have written** to you.（沒寫）／I **expected him to have helped** me with the work.（他沒幫）

## 🧷 不定詞的慣用表現

| 句型 | 意義 | 例 |
| --- | --- | --- |
| had better + 原形不定詞 | 最好（去做某事；had 為助動詞） | We **had better leave** it unsaid. |
| would rather + 原形 … than + 原形 | 寧願…而不願… | I **would rather stay** at home **than go** out. |
| do nothing but + 原形不定詞 | 什麼都不做只… | He **did nothing but eat** all day. |
| V + nothing but + to V | 除了…其他都不想（choose／want／expect／desire） | She **desires nothing but to live** peacefully. |
| cannot but + 原形／cannot help + V-ing／cannot help but + 原形 | 不得不、忍不住 | When I heard the news, I **couldn't but cry.** = couldn't help **crying** = couldn't help but **cry.** |
| have only to + 不定詞 | 只要做～就行 | You **have only to** follow his counsel. = All (that) you have to do is (to) follow… |
| used to + 不定詞 | 以前習慣～、過去經常～ | My father **used to smoke**, but now he doesn't. |

> [!WARNING]
> **`used to V`（以前習慣）vs `be used to V-ing`（現在習慣）**
> - used to + **原形**＝過去的習慣（現在沒有了）。
> - be used to + **V-ing**＝現在的習慣；此處 **to 是介系詞**：I am used to **staying** up late at night.

## ✳️ 特別注意：代不定詞／分離不定詞／獨立不定詞

### 代不定詞（只留 to，省動詞）
> 為避免相同動詞重複，句意明確時**只用 to、省略後面的原形動詞（連受詞）**，這個 to 稱代不定詞。

- You don't have to eat it if you don't want **to** (eat it).（❌ …if you don't want／口語 want to → wanna）
- A: How about coming to our house? B: I'd like **to** (come) if I won't be any trouble.

### 分離不定詞（to 與動詞間插入副詞）
> 修飾不定詞的副詞有時放在 to 與原形之間 → `to + 副詞 + V`。

- I wish **to utterly forget** my past.（= to forget my past **utterly**）
- She made up her mind **never to return.**（否定字 not／never 要放 `to V` **之前**，不可插中間；never 語氣強於 not）

### 獨立不定詞（修飾整句的慣用語，需逗號隔開）
> 只修飾整個主要子句、不管主詞是人或物；可置句首、句中或句尾。

- to speak strictly（嚴格來說）、to be frank (with you)（坦白說）、to begin with（一開始）、to tell the truth（老實說）、to be honest（誠實說）、to make matters worse（更糟的是）、to be sure（的確）、to be brief（簡單扼要地說）、to say nothing of～ = not to speak of = not to mention（更不用說～）、to do～justice（對～公平評判）、to say the least of it（保守地說）、strange to say（奇怪的是）、needless to say（無庸置疑地）、so to speak（可以說是）、not to say + 形容詞（即使不能說是～）
- **To begin with**, I'll tell you about my character.
- It was very cold, and **to make matters worse**, it began to rain.
- He can speak French and German, **to say nothing of** English.（= not to speak of／not to mention English）
- It would be foolish, **not to say crazy**, to say such a thing.（not to say + **形容詞**）

## ⚠️ 易錯點分析

> [!WARNING]
> **常見錯誤（皆為來源整理的重點）**
> - 不定詞當主詞**視為一件事 → 單數動詞**；太長改用**假主詞 it**。
> - `It's…to V` 意義主詞：修飾**事物**用 **for**、修飾**人（人格特質）**用 **of**（kind **of** you）。
> - **否定不定詞用 `not／never to V`**，不加助動詞；`not/never` 放 `to V` **之前**，不可插進 to 與動詞之間（分離不定詞只插一般副詞）。
> - **疑問詞 + to V** 是名詞片語（可主／受／補）；疑問詞後碰到動詞一定用不定詞。
> - 形容詞用法**後位修飾**，動詞片語的**介系詞別漏**（parents to take care **of**、a knife to cut it **with**）。
> - **感官／使役動詞**後用**原形不定詞**；但**被動語態要還原成 to 不定詞**（were watched **to cross**、were made **to try**）；使役被動只有 **make**。
> - **too … to** 已含否定「不能」，受詞與主詞相同時要**省略**（too small to sleep in，不加 it）。
> - **prove／turn out 只接不定詞**；It 假主詞句型只有 turn out 有。
> - **助動詞 + have + p.p.** 對過去推測／未實現：can't/couldn't（否定推測）、must（肯定推測）、should/ought to（未實現、帶責備）。
> - **`used to V`（過去習慣）≠ `be used to V-ing`（現在習慣，to 是介系詞）**。

- [ ] Q1：填 for／of：It's very kind ___ you to help me；It's dangerous ___ children to play on the streets.
- [ ] Q2：把 To answer this question is difficult for me. 改用假主詞 it。
- [ ] Q3：改錯：The mother made the boy to turn off the TV.（主動）／被動又該怎麼寫？
- [ ] Q4：合併成 too…to：This bed is very small. I can't sleep in it.
- [ ] Q5：用完成式不定詞改寫，表示「更早」：It seemed that he had spent the money.（He seemed …）
- [ ] Q6：辨析 `used to smoke` 與 `be used to smoking` 的差別。
- [ ] Q7：把 not／never 放對位置：She made up her mind ___ return.（下定決心絕不再回來）

<details>
<summary>✅ 解答</summary>

A1：kind 修飾「人」→ **of** you；dangerous 修飾「事物」→ **for** children。
A2：**It is** difficult **for me to answer** this question.
A3：感官／使役後用原形 → The mother made the boy **turn off** the TV.；被動只有 make 要用 to → The boy **was made to turn off** the TV.
A4：This bed is **too small for me to sleep in.**（受詞 it 省略）
A5：He seemed **to have spent** the money.
A6：**used to smoke**＝過去的習慣（現在不抽了）；**be used to smoking**＝現在習慣抽菸（to 是介系詞，接 V-ing）。
A7：否定字放 to V 之前 → She made up her mind **never to return.**
