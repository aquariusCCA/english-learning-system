# 疑問

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **一句話核心**
> 疑問詞（WH~ 及 how）為首＝WH 問句。疑問詞三分：**疑問代名詞**（what／who／which／whose，可當主／受／補）、**疑問副詞**（when／where／why／how，**後面不可直接接動詞**）、**疑問形容詞**（what／which + 名詞）。**疑問詞當主詞 → 視為單數**。**間接問句**：疑問詞移入句中，其後改「**S + V**」肯定語序（無疑問詞的 Yes/No 問句改用 **if／whether**）。

## 📊 疑問詞三分

| 類別 | 疑問詞 | 特性 |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **疑問代名詞** | what、who、which、whose | 當名詞 → 可當主詞、受詞、補語 |
| **疑問副詞** | when、where、why、how | 當副詞 → **後面不可直接接動詞** |
| **疑問形容詞** | what、which | 後接名詞（what/which + 名詞） |

---

## 🔤 疑問代名詞（可當主詞／補語／受詞）

### 當主詞（`疑問詞 + 動詞…?`）
> [!WARNING]
> **疑問詞當主詞時視為單數，其後接單數動詞（「狀況不明視為單數」）。**

- **Who is** the captain of this ship? — Jack Brown **is**.（who 當主詞）
- **What makes** you laugh so?／**Who is** singing? — Mary and Helen **are**.（問句用 is；答句知道人數用 are）
- **Which of** those ships **is** the May Flower?

### 當補語（`疑問詞 + be 動詞 + 主詞…?`）
- **Who is** the captain? — **It's** Jack Brown.（who 當主詞補語）
- **Whose** keys are these?（whose + 名詞 → 所有格）／**Whose** are these keys?（whose 單獨 → 所有代名詞）

### 當受詞（`疑問詞 + be/助動詞 + 主詞…?`）
- **What** does she have for breakfast?／**Which** of these singers do you like?
- **Who(m)** are you planning to invite?（受格 whom，m 常省；口語常用 Who）
- **What's** the weather **like**?（= How's the weather?；like 是介系詞 → 用 what）

### what 的慣用表現
| 表現 | 意義 | 例 |
| --- | --- | --- |
| What about + V-ing | …如何？（= how about = what do you say） | **What about** going for a walk? |
| What … for? | 為什麼（= why） | **What** did you do that **for?** |
| What … like? | …如何／像什麼 | **What** does it look **like?** |
| what not | 種種其他 | butter, sugar and **what not** |
| What do you say to + V-ing | …如何？ | **What do you say to** playing basketball? |
| What if…? | 萬一…怎麼辦 | **What if** it rains? |
| What will become of…? | …會變怎樣 | **What will become of** the whales…? |
| I know what. | 我有個好主意（= I have an idea） | |
| What did I tell you? | 沒錯吧！（帶埋怨或確認） | |

## 📍 疑問副詞（when／where／why／how）
> 副詞不能當主詞、受詞 → 疑問副詞後**不可直接接動詞**（要接問句句型）。

- **Where** are you from?／**When** did you hear it?／**Why** didn't you lock the door?／**How** did you get here?
- **How** 也可問程度、感覺：**How** do you feel? — fine.／How **old** will you be?（疑問副詞後可加形容詞）

### 詢問程度 `How + 形容詞／副詞…?`
> how deep／far／fast／high／long／many／much／old／tall／thick／wide／soon…

- **How far** is it…?／**How long** is this bridge?
- ⚠️ **fast（速度）vs soon（時間）**：How **fast** can you run?／How **soon** will he come back?
- ⚠️ **high（物體）vs tall（人）**；**many（可數）vs much（不可數）**：how **many** dollars／how **much** money。

### how 的慣用表現
- **How about + V-ing**（如何，about 是介系詞）／**How are you**（熟人）vs **How do you do**（初次見面）／**How come + S + V**（怎麼會…，其後用肯定語序）。

### 強調疑問的程度（放疑問詞後）
- **-ever**：**Whoever** said so?／**However** did you do it?（究竟、到底）
- **on earth／in the world**：Where **on earth** has he gone?／What **in the world** are you doing?

---

## 🏷️ 疑問形容詞（只有 what／which）
> what／which + 名詞（放名詞前，具形容詞性）。

- **What nationality** is that man?／**Which color** do you prefer, red or blue?

---

## 🔀 疑問詞位置不同者（表特殊語氣）
> 疑問詞不在句首、放句中，多表達語氣而非真正發問。

- I told him the truth. — You said **what?**（你說什麼？＝驚訝語氣）
- I broke your… — You broke my **what?**

---

## 🗣️ 敘述句型的疑問句
> 用肯定／否定句型、**句尾語調上揚**即成疑問。

- You gave him your telephone number?／This sword is a thousand years old?

---

## 🔁 間接問句
> 疑問詞移入句中 → 其後改「**主詞 + 動詞**」肯定語序；句尾標點以**主要子句**為準。

- I don't know. + Who is the man…? → I don't know **who the man … is.**
- Can you tell me…? + Why is the man…? → Can you tell me **why the man is** wearing a tuxedo?
- ⚠️ 疑問詞本身當主詞時，其後可直接接動詞：I have no idea **who broke** the window.

### Yes／No 問句 → 用 if／whether（是否）
> Yes/No 問句沒有疑問詞，變間接問句會失去「嗎」→ 補 **if／whether**（連接詞）。

- Please tell me **whether／if** the museum is open on Monday.
- I don't know **whether or not** she is married.（= whether she is married **or not**）
- He can't decide **whether to visit** her or not.（主詞相同可省，助動詞省 → whether + to V）

### `疑問詞 + do you think + S + V`（think 類動詞）
- Do you know **what** I have…? — **Yes, I do.**（Yes/No 問句）
- **What** do you think I have…? — I think you have cherries.（think/believe/guess/suppose/imagine/say/expect… → 疑問詞提前，**不能**答 yes/no）

## ➰ 附加問句
> 依附直述句／祈使句尾，詢問或徵求同意。

> [!WARNING]
> **直述句附加問句三原則**
> 1. 肯定句 → 否定附加；否定句 → 肯定附加。
> 2. be／助動詞依前句判斷，否定附加要**縮寫**。
> 3. 主詞用**人稱代名詞**。

- Mary doesn't like onions, **does she?**／The victim could **hardly** walk, **could he?**（hardly 否定字 → 後用肯定）
- **am** 特例：I am your boss, **aren't I?**（或 am I not／ain't I，am+not 無縮寫）。
- **There + be** 開頭：附加問句主詞仍用 there：There are ten more days…, **aren't there?**

### 祈使句的附加問句（不適用三原則）
- 一般命令 → **will you?**（Don't touch it, **will you?**）；更客氣 → **can't you?**
- 邀請（Let's 除外）→ 一般 will you？，更客氣 → **won't you?**（Take a seat, **won't you?**）

---

## ❓ 修辭疑問句
> 帶反問語氣、不一定要回答（正話反說）。

- **Who knows?**（= Nobody knows.）／**What is the use?**（= It is no use.）／**What difference does it make?**（= It makes no difference.）／**Isn't it fantastic?**（= It is very fantastic.）／**Shall I ever forget her?**（= I shall never forget her.）

---

## 💬 應答疑問句
> 用問句句型（be/助動詞 + 主詞）回應對方，表「真的嗎？」。

- I'm not interested in video games. — **Aren't you?**
- He hit a home run yesterday. — Oh, **did he?**

---

## ⚠️ 易錯點分析

> [!WARNING]
> **常見錯誤（皆為來源整理的重點）**
> - **疑問詞當主詞 → 單數動詞**（Who is singing?）；本身當主詞時後可直接接動詞。
> - **疑問副詞後不可直接接動詞**（要接問句句型）；**疑問代名詞**可直接接動詞（它就是主詞）。
> - **間接問句改「S + V」肯定語序**（what time **it is**，非 what time is it）；標點以主要子句為準。
> - **Yes/No 問句** → 間接問句補 **if／whether**；think 類動詞 → 疑問詞提前、不可答 yes/no。
> - **附加問句三原則**（肯定↔否定、縮寫、人稱代名詞）；**am → aren't I**；**there** 保留；否定字（hardly…）算否定句。
> - **祈使句附加問句**用 will you／won't you／can't you（不套三原則）。
> - **fast/soon、high/tall、many/much、what…like/how** 別混。
