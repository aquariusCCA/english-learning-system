# 特殊構句（倒裝／強調／省略／插入／同位語）

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **一句話核心**
> 為了**強調語氣**或**精簡表達**，英文可打破正常語序：**倒裝**（動詞/助動詞移到主詞前）、**強調**（It…that、do/does/did、字詞重複…）、**省略**（略去重複或雙方已知的字）、**插入**（逗號/破折號夾入補充語）、**同位語**（名詞後補充等值名詞）。

---

## 🔀 一、倒裝

> 正常語序「主詞 + 動詞」→ 倒裝改成「**動詞/助動詞 + 主詞**」。三種形式：① **V + S**（be 動詞、方向副詞後）② **do/does/did + S + 原形**（一般動詞）③ **助動詞（will/can/should/have…）+ S + 原形/p.p.**。

### 觸發倒裝的情形
| 觸發 | 例 |
| --- | --- |
| **否定字放句首**（never、hardly、rarely、seldom、little…；本身有否定意、不與 not 併用） | **Never** is he late.／**Rarely** does he tell a joke.／**Never** have I seen such scenery. |
| **hardly/scarcely～when/before、no sooner～than**（一…就…） | **Hardly** had I sat down **when** someone knocked.／**No sooner** had he reached home **than** it began to rain. |
| **含否定字的片語** | **In no circumstances** will I allow you to go.／**Not only** does he draw **but** he writes novels. |
| **Not until + 副詞子句 + 倒裝**（倒裝在後面的主要子句） | **Not until** I talked to him **did I know** he was homesick. |
| **否定字 + 受詞放句首**（受詞跟著搬） | **No hope** did I have.／**Not a word** did my father say. |
| **Only 近乎否定**（only + 副詞/子句放句首） | **Only** in case of emergency **can we** use this exit.／**Only if** you pass, **will I** accept you. |
| **方向/場所副詞放句首** | **Here comes** the teacher.／**Over the wall came** a shower of stones. |
| **程度副詞放句首**（well、so…that、such…that） | **Well do I** remember it.／**So beautiful is** Amily **that**…／**Such a boring lecture was it that**… |
| **補語放句首** | **Happy is** the country that has no war.／**Faint grew** the sound of the bell. |
| **so/neither/nor 開頭**（附和「也（不）」） | Amy watched TV, and **so did** her sister.／You don't want to, and **neither do** I. |

> [!WARNING]
> **倒裝易錯點**
> - **一般動詞**不可直接移到主詞前 → 借 **do/does/did**（Never **does he go**…）；動詞是 have/助動詞則直接移（Never **have I** seen）。
> - **否定字放句首「一定」要倒裝**；不含否定字的受詞放句首（強調對照）**不倒裝**：That red car I **like** very much.
> - **主詞為代名詞時不倒裝**（方向副詞、補語句型）：Here **he comes**.／Happy **he is**.／Right **you are**.
> - **that/when 等後面子句無否定字 → 照寫、不倒裝**（只有含否定字的子句才倒裝）。
> - **If only + 假設句**（要是…就好了）≠ Only if；If only 不倒裝：**If only** I had money!

## ❗ 二、強調

### It + be + 強調部分 + that + 剩餘（分裂句）
> 可強調**主詞、受詞、地方、時間**；📛 **不能**強調動詞、形容詞、副詞。**that 不可省**。

- 原句：My father caught a carp in this river last week.
- 強調人：It was **my father that (who)** caught a carp…
- 強調物：It was **a carp that (which)** my father caught…
- 強調地：It was **in this river that (where)**…／強調時：It was **last week that (when)**…

### 其他強調法
| 對象 | 方法 | 例 |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **動詞** | do／does／did + 原形（肯定句中） | I **do** love you.／when she **does** speak… |
| **名詞** | 反身代名詞／very／all | the **very** spot／He is happiness **itself**（= all happiness） |
| **形容詞/副詞原級** | very、so、awfully、terribly、dreadfully | The dog is **terribly** smart. |
| **比較級** | much、even、a lot、far、still（＝…得多） | much **easier** than I had thought |
| **最高級** | much、far and away、by far、possible | **much** the best plan／the lowest **possible** prices |
| **疑問詞** | on earth、in the world（口語 the hell/devil） | Who **on earth** is calling?／What **in the world**…? |
| **否定** | 否定字句首／at all／far from／in the least／by no means／anything but | He is **not a liar at all**. = He is **far from** / **by no means** / **anything but** a liar. |
| **重複字詞** | A and A | hours **and** hours／again **and** again |

> ⚠️ **at all**：否定句＝「絕不」；疑問/條件句＝「究竟、既然」（Did you follow it **at all**?／If you learn English **at all**, learn it well.）。
> ⚠️ **All (that) you have to do is + 原形**（先行詞 all → 關代用 that、可省；動詞用單數；be 後接原形）：All I have to do **is get** on the Internet.

## ✂️ 三、省略

> 省去一個（或幾個）字而無損表達，多因**與前面重複**或**雙方已知**。

| 情形 | 例（括號＝省略） |
| --- | --- |
| **重複的詞類** | the boys were not (brave)／Cathy ate a hamburger, and I (ate) French fries／if you want to (go home) |
| **所有格後已知地點** | from my uncle's (home) |
| **連綴動詞前的 it/that** | (It) **Sounds** great.／(He) **Doesn't look** well.／(I) **Don't know** what to say. |
| **從屬子句主詞＝主要子句主詞** → 省主詞（動詞一併省） | When (they were) in school…／Though (he is) poor…／If (meat is) put in the fridge… |
| **if any／if possible**（省的主詞動詞與主句不同） | …if (there are) any.／…if (it is) possible. |
| **感嘆句的主詞+動詞** | How careless (it is) of you…!／What a pity (it is) that…! |
| **受格關係代名詞** | the key (that) you are looking for |
| **連接詞 that** | I believe (that) he is…；建議/要求/命令後 that **不可省**、should 可省：I suggested that Jack (should) **finish**… |
| **廣告/告示/標題** | (The) HOUSE FOR SALE／(This) Road (is) Closed／Kidnapper (was) Arrested |

> ⚠️ 連接兩個 that 子句時，**第一個 that 可省、第二個不可省**（離主要動詞太遠，省了會誤解歸屬）：He said (that) his brother was out, but **that** his sister was home.

## 💬 四、插入

> 用**逗號或破折號**把字、片語、子句夾入句中（或句尾），補充說明或加強語氣；拿掉後句子仍完整。

| 層級 | 常見插入語 | 例 |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **字** | fortunately、undoubtedly、nevertheless（連接副詞，兼連接詞性） | His son, **fortunately**, was rescued.／She was tired; **nevertheless**, she went to work. |
| **片語** | after all（畢竟）、in my opinion（依我之見）、in short（簡言之）、if ever、to make matters worse（＝what was worse，更糟的是） | Clothes here, **in my opinion**, are overpriced.／She seldom, **if ever**, goes alone. |
| **子句** | as it is（事實上）、as it were＝so to speak（所謂的）、as far as I know（就我所知）、I think、as you know | He is, **as it were**, a walking dictionary.／Too much exercise, **I think**, is bad for you. |

## 🟰 五、同位語

> 在名詞/代名詞後放另一個名詞（或名詞相等語）補充說明，兩者**地位相同、指同一對象**（是「強調地位相同」的插入）。

| 型態 | 例 |
| --- | --- |
| 名詞/名字 | Her best friend**, Lisa,** is a nurse.（Lisa = friend）／You**, students,** have to study hard. |
| a friend of mine 等 | Carter**, a friend of mine,** graduated from Oxford. |
| of + 名詞 | the city **of** New York（of New York = city） |
| **同位語子句**（whether／that，與前面名詞可互換） | the question **whether he would agree**…／the fact **that she was trying to conceal the scandal**（fact = that 子句） |
| or／that is (to say)／namely（也就是） | It is a fiddle, **or** a violin.／…**that is,** about nine o'clock. |

> ⚠️ **同位語 that 子句 vs 形容詞子句 that**：
> - **同位語**：the fact **that she was trying to conceal the scandal**（that 子句 = fact，兩者相等、可互換）。
> - **形容詞子句**：the fact **that she was trying to conceal**（that 只代替 fact 當受詞：conceal **the fact**）。

## ⚠️ 易錯點分析

> [!WARNING]
> **常見錯誤（皆為來源整理的重點）**
> - **倒裝**：一般動詞借 do/does/did；否定字/only 句首必倒裝；**代名詞主詞不倒裝**；含否定字的子句才倒裝。
> - **Not until + 子句 + 倒裝**（倒裝在主要子句，非 until 子句）。
> - **It…that** 分裂句不能強調動詞/形容詞/副詞、that 不可省；**do/does/did** 強調動詞。
> - 強調程度：原級 very、**比較級 much/even/far**、最高級 much/by far/possible。
> - **at all**：否定＝絕不、疑問/條件＝究竟/既然。
> - 省略：主詞相同才省從屬子句主詞（動詞一併省）；**建議/要求/命令的 that 不可省、should 可省**；連兩個 that 子句「第一可省、第二不可省」。
> - **同位語 that（= 名詞，可互換）vs 形容詞子句 that（只代替名詞當受詞）**。
