# 關係詞

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **一句話核心**
> 關係詞 = **連接詞 + (代名詞／副詞／形容詞)**，引導**形容詞子句**修飾前面的**先行詞**。三類：**關係代名詞**（who／whose／whom、which、that、含先行詞的 what）、**關係副詞**（where／when／why／how）、**關係形容詞**（what／which + 名詞）；另有準關代 **as／but／than** 與 **複合關係詞（+ever＝任何／無論）**。

## 📊 關係代名詞：種類與格

| 先行詞 | 主格（+動詞） | 所有格（+名詞） | 受格（+主詞+動詞） |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 人 | who | whose | whom |
| 事、物、動物 | which | whose／of which | which |
| 人／事物／動物 | that | — | that |
| **含先行詞** | what | — | what |

> **判斷小技巧**：關代前畫 `{`、由句尾往前找第一個動詞在其前畫 `}`；括號外若是完整句子就對了。

## 📐 用法
- **主格**（後接動詞）：I have a friend **who** likes music.／English is a language **which** is spoken all over the world.
- **所有格**（後接名詞）：The student **whose** mother died…／a car **whose**（= the engine **of which**）engine was good.（無生物所有格 `A of B`）
- **受格**（後接主詞+動詞，**須拿到先行詞後、可省略**）：the friends **(whom)** I hadn't seen；the parcel **(which)** we received.
- ⚠️ 受格關代拿到先行詞後、**緊跟先行詞**（不可留在動詞後）。

## ✂️ 關係代名詞的省略
| 可省略情形 | 例 |
| --- | --- |
| **受格關代** | The lady **(whom)** we met is our teacher. |
| **當補語的主格關代**（後接 be 動詞） | She is not the cheerful girl **(that)** she was. |
| There／Here is(are) + 關代 | There is someone **(who)** wants to see you. |
| 關代後接 There is… | the only bow tie **(that)** there is in this store |

## 🔗 關係代名詞 + 介系詞
> 受格關代所引導的子句若含介系詞，介系詞可**後置**或**前置**到關代前。

- the man **(whom／that)** you lent the money **to** = the man **to whom** you lent the money.
- ⚠️ **介系詞前置時**：關代**不可用 that**、**不可省略**、人用 whom／物用 which（with whom、for which）。
- ⚠️ **動詞／形容詞 + 介系詞的片語**（do with＝戒掉），介系詞**只能後置**：the habit which you must do **with**（不可 with which）。

## 🎯 只能用 that 的情形
> 口訣：**最高級、序數、人+物同時、避免重複（先行詞前有疑問詞 who/which）**；**the only／very／same**；**all／no／every／any／~thing／~one**。

- the **coldest** day **(that)** we've experienced／the **first** thing **(that)** you have to do。
- the driver and the car **that** had fallen…（人+物）／**Who that** has common sense…（避免 who who）。
- the **only** man **(that)** I love／**All that** you see／**anything that** needed repairing。

## 🟰 限定用法 vs 非限定用法
| | 限定用法 | 非限定用法（補述） |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 逗號 | 無 | **有逗號** |
| that 代替 | 可 | **不可** |
| 受格關代省略 | 可 | **不可** |
| 語調 | 先行詞語調上揚 ↗ | 先行詞語調下降 ↘ |
| 含義 | We have two daughters **who** became nurses.（不只兩個女兒） | We have two daughters**, who** became nurses.（只有兩個女兒） |

- 非限定用法 = **連接詞 + 代名詞**（and／but／because + 代名詞）：I bought a hat**, which**（= but it）was too big.／**, who**（= and/because he）…。
- which 在非限定用法可指**前面整個子句**：He tore up my photo**, which**（= and it）upset me.

## 🌐 what（含先行詞）
> what = the thing(s) which = all that = anything that；**看到 what 前面不會有先行詞**。

- **What** I want is peace of mind.（= The thing which I want…）／told them **what**（= all that）they wanted to know.
- ⚠️ **what 當關代**（無中文「什麼」）vs **當疑問詞**（譯「什麼」）：I don't know **what** he is.（問職業→疑問詞）。

### what 的慣用表現
| 句型 | 意義 | 例 |
| --- | --- | --- |
| what is called／what we call | 所謂的 | He is **what is called** a walking dictionary. |
| A is to B what C is to D | A 之於 B 猶如 C 之於 D | Air is to us **what** water is to fish. |
| what A is（was／used to be） | 今日（昔日）的 A | She is not **what she was** ten years ago. |
| what is more／what is worse | 更好的是／更糟的是 | …, **what is more**, he is kindhearted. |
| what with A and (what with) B | 一則因…一則因…（列舉壞事） | **What with** overwork and poor meals, she fell ill. |

## 📍 關係副詞（= 連接詞 + 副詞，代替「介系詞 + 名詞」）
| 關係副詞 | 先行詞 | 例 |
| --- | --- | --- |
| where | 場所（case／point／situation 亦可） | This is the place **where** I met my wife. |
| when | 時間 | Sunday is the day **when** I have no school. |
| why | 理由（先行詞 reason） | the reason **why** he was absent |
| how | 方法（**無明確先行詞**） | This is **how** he solved it.（= the way **that**；不可 the way how） |

- **先行詞常可省略**：This is (the reason) **why** I left early.（reason／why 三種：留 why 省 reason／留 reason 省 why／都留）。
- **that 可代替關係副詞**（多代 when，可省）：the day **(that)** I first met her。
- where／when 也有**非限定用法**：She moved to New York**, where** she opened a shop.

## 🔁 複合關係詞（關係詞 + ever）
> 兩義：**任何（= any…）** 或 **無論（讓步，= no matter…）**，由前後文判斷。

| 複合關係詞 | ＝ | 例 |
| --- | --- | --- |
| whoever（whomever） | anyone who／no matter who | help **whoever** was in need |
| whichever | any one that | to **whichever** you want |
| whatever | anything that／no matter what | order **whatever** you like／**Whatever** happens, I'll love you |
| wherever／whenever | no matter where／when | thinking of you **wherever** I am |
| however + 形容詞/副詞 | no matter how | **However fast** you walk…（副詞緊跟所修飾詞） |

## 🏷️ 關係形容詞與準關係代名詞
### 關係形容詞（= 連接詞 + 形容詞）
- **what + 名詞 = all the + 名詞 + that**：I gave the child **what little money** I had.（我僅有的零錢）。
- **介系詞 + which + 名詞**：…during **which time**…／in **which case** we'll be late.

### as／but／than 當準關係代名詞（各帶特殊語氣）
| 詞 | 用於 | 例 |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **as**（如同、像） | the same／such／so 之後、指整句 | She wears the same earrings **as** I do.／**As is often the case**, he came late. |
| **but**（= that…not，雙重否定） | 否定句 | There is no rule **but** has exceptions.（凡事都有例外） |
| **than**（比較） | 比較句 | You did more work **than** I had expected. |

## ⚠️ 易錯點分析

> [!WARNING]
> **常見錯誤（皆為來源整理的重點）**
> - **關代 = 連接詞 + 代名詞**；主格後接動詞、所有格後接名詞、受格後接主詞+動詞。
> - **受格關代可省略**，且要**緊跟先行詞**；當補語的主格關代亦可省。
> - **介系詞前置**時：關代不可用 that、不可省、人用 whom／物用 which；片語動詞（do with）的介系詞只能後置。
> - **只能用 that**：最高級／序數／人+物／先行詞前有疑問詞／the only·very·same／all·no·every·any·~thing。
> - **非限定用法（有逗號）不可用 that、不可省受格關代**；which 可指整個子句。
> - **what 含先行詞**（前面無先行詞）、**當關代不譯「什麼」**；the way 不可配 how。
> - **關係副詞代替「介系詞+名詞」**（where=at which…）；先行詞常可省。
> - **複合關係詞 +ever = 任何／無論**（= no matter）。

- [ ] Q1：填關代：I have a friend ___ mother is a singer.
- [ ] Q2：介系詞前置改寫：the man whom you spoke of → the man ___ ___ you spoke.
- [ ] Q3：為什麼只能用 that：Today is the coldest day ___ we've experienced.
- [ ] Q4：辨析：a son who lives in Taipei（無逗號）vs a son, who lives in Taipei（有逗號）。
- [ ] Q5：用 what 改寫：The thing which I need is time. →
- [ ] Q6：複合關係詞：___ happens, I'll support you.（無論發生什麼）

<details>
<summary>✅ 解答</summary>

A1：後接名詞 mother → 所有格 **whose**。
A2：the man **of whom** you spoke（介系詞前置、whom 不可省不可 that）。
A3：先行詞前有最高級 → 只能用 **that**（且受格可省）。
A4：無逗號＝限定（不只一個兒子）；有逗號＝非限定補述（只有一個兒子）。
A5：**What** I need is time.
A6：**Whatever** happens, I'll support you.（= No matter what）。

</details>
