# 關係代名詞

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **一句話核心**
> 關係代名詞 = **連接詞 + 代名詞**，引導**形容詞子句**修飾前面的**先行詞**（要緊跟先行詞）。依「先行詞是人或物」與「格（主／受／所有）」選 **who／whom／which／whose／that**。**受格關代可省略**；**that 有特定使用時機**；**加逗號＝非限定（補述）用法**。

---

## 🧭 功用：連接詞 + 代名詞
- I have a friend. The friend lives in Paris. → I have a friend **and he** lives in Paris. → I have a friend **who** lives in Paris.
  - **who** ＝把兩句連起來（連接詞）＋代替 he（代名詞）；**a friend ＝先行詞**（重複的名詞先出現者）。
  - 「住在巴黎的朋友」→ a friend **who lives in Paris**（who lives in Paris 是**形容詞子句**，後位修飾 a friend）。
- 關代所引導的形容詞子句要**緊跟先行詞**（被別的修飾語隔開才例外，如 an old man **in the apartment** who…）。

---

## 📊 關係代名詞的種類

| 先行詞 | 主格 | 所有格 | 受格 |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 人 | who | whose | whom |
| 事物、動物 | which | whose | which |
| 人／事物／動物 | **that** | ✗ | **that** |

**格的用法**：主格 **+ 動詞**（the man **who talked** to me）／所有格 **+ 名詞**（the man **whose car** was stolen）／受格 **+ 主詞 + 動詞 +（介系詞）**（the man **whom I met**）。
> whom 口語中常用 who 代替。

---

## 🔹 主格關代（who／which／that + 動詞）
- **who（人）**：The man **who wears** sunglasses is my father.
- **which（事物／動物）**：I live in the house **which stands** on the hill.／English is a language **which is spoken** all over the world.
- **that（人／物／動物皆可）**：People **that help** doctors… are called nurses.／I like the building **that was designed** by Mr. Wu.

> [!WARNING]
> **主格關代後的動詞須與先行詞的單複數一致**：Tom is one of the boys **who are** fond of sports.／children **who tell** lies（先行詞 children 複數 → tell 不加 s）。

> **that 的其他身分**：指示代名詞（…hotter than **that** in Taipei）、指示形容詞（Look at **that** dog）、連接詞（I think **that**…）。關代 that 前面一定有**先行詞**、且有代替名詞的功用——用這點與連接詞 that 區分。

---

## 🔸 受格關代（whom／which／that + 主詞 + 動詞 +（介系詞））
> 受格關代要拿到主詞、動詞前，緊跟先行詞。

- **whom（人）**：The man **who(m) she married** is a teacher.
- **which（物）**：Here is a magazine **which I borrowed** from Miss Wang.／Remember the advice **which I gave** you.
- **that**：The little girl **that my dad is talking to** is very cute.／The sandwiches **that we ate** for lunch were not delicious.

---

## 🔻 所有格關代（whose + 名詞）
> 先行詞（人／事物／動物）+ **whose + 名詞**。

- I know a girl **whose father** is a journalist.
- Look at the house **whose roof** was damaged.（**whose roof** 整個才是子句主詞，故不用 which）

---

## ⚠️ 特別注意

### 受格關代可省略
> **只有受格關代可省略。**
- Soccer is the sport **(which／that) I like** (the) best.

### 介系詞 + 受格關代
- 介系詞在句尾時：關代可用 who／whom／that，也可**省略**：The woman **you are speaking of**…
- **介系詞提前到關代前**時：**不可用 that、不可省略**，先行詞人用 **whom**、物用 **which**：The woman **of whom** you are speaking…／The train **for which** I am waiting…

### 關係代名詞只用 that 的時機
> [!TIP]
> **口訣：①最高級、序數、人＋物、避免重複　②all／no／every／any／the～**

1. 先行詞前有**最高級**：Today is the coldest day **(that)** we've ever experienced.
2. 先行詞前有**序數**：Kenny was the first boy **that** rushed out of the classroom.
3. 先行詞**人與物同時出現**：the boy and his dog **that** are coming this way.
4. 先行詞前有**疑問詞 who／which**（避免 who who／which which 重複）：Who **that** has seen…?／Which was the bag **that** you left…?
5. 先行詞有 **all／no／every／any／～thing／～one**：All **(that)** students have to do is study hard.（all that = what）／everything **that** a woman could wish for.
6. 先行詞前有 **the only／the same／the very**：the only one **that** trusted me／the very novel **(that)** I've looked for.

## 🔀 限定用法 vs 非限定用法

| | 限定用法 | 非限定用法（補述） |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 逗號 | 無 | **有** |
| 可否用 that | ✅ 可 | ❌ 不可 |
| 受格關代省略 | ✅ 可 | ❌ 不可 |
| 語調 | 上揚 ↗ | 下降 ↘ |

- They had two daughters **who** became actresses.（未提女兒總數，不只兩個）
- They had two daughters**, who** became actresses.（明確只有兩個女兒）
- 💡 **小技巧**：逗號後的 who 直接翻「他／她／他們」（My brother**, who** is a dentist, is studying in the US. → 只有一個哥哥）。

## 🔗 關係代名詞的同義表現（可換成分詞／介系詞片語）
- **先行詞 + 主格關代 + 動詞 → 名詞 + 現在分詞（主動）**：a notice **that says** "No parking" = a notice **saying** "No parking".
- **先行詞 + 主格關代 + be + p.p. → 名詞 + 過去分詞（被動）**：a cake **which was made** by Mrs. White = a cake **made** by Mrs. White.
- **先行詞 + who + have/has（有）→ 名詞 + with／having**：a girl **who has** blond hair = a girl **with**／**having** blond hair.

---

## 🧩 修飾名詞的字／片語／子句（統整）
- **字**：形容詞／現在分詞（主動）／過去分詞（被動）多**前位**（a **red**／**blooming**／**fallen** flower）；～thing／～body + 形容詞為**後位**（something **interesting**）。
- **片語**（後位）：名詞 + 介系詞／分詞／不定詞片語（the pen **on the desk**、a cat **sleeping on the chair**、the window **broken by Tom**、a pen **to write with**）。
- **子句**（後位）：名詞 + 關係代名詞子句。
- **三者可互換**：the lady **who wears** a dress = the lady **wearing** a dress = the lady **in** a dress.

## ⚠️ 易錯點分析

> [!WARNING]
> **常見錯誤（皆為來源整理的重點）**
> - **主格關代後動詞與先行詞單複數一致**（boys who **are**…）。
> - **受格關代可省略**（限定用法）；介系詞**提前**時關代**不可用 that、不可省略**。
> - **that 專用時機**：最高級／序數／人＋物／疑問詞後／all·no·every·any·~thing／the only·same·very。
> - **非限定用法（逗號）不可用 that、不可省略**。
> - **whose + 名詞**整個當子句主詞（whose roof was damaged）。
> - **同義表現**：主格+動詞→V-ing、主格+be+p.p.→p.p.、who+have→with／having。

## 🧠 自我測驗　💬 AI 補充
> 複習時作答，答完再看下方答案。（此區為 AI 出題，非來源內容）

- [ ] Q1：合併：I know a boy. His father is a doctor.（用 whose）
- [ ] Q2：合併：This is the book. I bought it yesterday.（受格，並說明可否省略關代）
- [ ] Q3：填空：He is the tallest boy ___ I have ever seen.（填關代並說明為何）
- [ ] Q4：這句是限定還非限定？My mother, who loves cooking, made this cake.
- [ ] Q5：把 a boy who is standing there 換成分詞寫法。

<details>
<summary>✅ 解答</summary>

A1：I know a boy **whose** father is a doctor.
A2：This is the book **(which／that)** I bought yesterday.（受格關代 → **可省略**）。
A3：先行詞前有**最高級**（tallest）→ 只用 **that**（可省略）：the tallest boy **(that)** I have ever seen.
A4：有逗號 → **非限定（補述）用法**（我只有一位母親）。
A5：主格關代 + 動詞 → 現在分詞：a boy **standing** there.

</details>
