# 附加問句

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **一句話核心**
> 附加問句依附在直述句／祈使句尾，用來**詢問**或**徵求同意**。**三原則**：①肯定直述句→**否定**附加問句（反之亦然）；②附加問句的 be／助動詞**依前面直述句判斷**，且**否定附加必縮寫**；③附加問句主詞用**人稱代名詞**。語調**上升↗＝詢問**、**下降↘＝尋求認同**。

---

## 🧭 簡介
- 語調**上升↗＝詢問事物**（等於一般問句，可 Yes/No 回答）：You are from Japan, **aren't you**?↗（= Are you from Japan?）
- 語調**下降↘＝心中已有定見、尋求認同**：You should follow traffic rules, **shouldn't you**?↘

---

## 📐 三原則

### ① 肯否相反
肯定直述句 → **否定**附加問句；否定直述句 → **肯定**附加問句。

### ② 動詞依直述句判斷（否定附加**必縮寫**）
| 直述句 | 附加問句 |
| --- | --- |
| be 動詞 | be 動詞 |
| 一般動詞 | 助動詞 **do／does／did** |
| 助動詞 will／can／should… | 同一助動詞 |

### ③ 附加問句主詞用**人稱代名詞**
| 直述句主詞 | 附加問句主詞 |
| --- | --- |
| Tom、John… | he |
| Mary、Amy… | she |
| Tom and Mary | they |
| this、that | **it** |
| baby、child | **it** |
| 不定詞、動名詞 | **it** |

- 人稱優先序：第一人稱 > 第二 > 第三（我＋你/Tom → **we**；你＋Tom → **you**）。

**例句**：It's cold and humid, **isn't it**?／Dad was good at sports, **wasn't he**?／People can't use fake credit cards, **can they**?／Eating too much junk food isn't good for health, **is it**?（動名詞主詞 → it）

---

## ⚠️ 特別注意（特例）

- **am**：附加問句用 **aren't I**（或 am I not／老式 ain't I）：I am the right man, **aren't I**?
- **There is／are…**：附加問句主詞仍用 **there**：There is an old man jogging…, **isn't there**?／There are not any pedestrians…, **are there**?
- **have／has／had 要分助動詞或一般動詞**：
  - have/has + p.p.（助動詞）、had better + 原形（助動詞）→ 用該助動詞：He's decided to quit…, **hasn't he**?／You had better keep silent…, **hadn't you**?
  - **have to**（have 是一般動詞）→ 用 **do**：We have to finish the work…, **don't we**?
  - **he's** 可能是 he **is** 或 he **has**，看後文：He's fond of pop music（is）, **isn't he**?／He's decided…（has，完成式）, **hasn't he**?
- **直述句含否定字**（no、nothing、never、seldom、little、few…）→ 用**肯定**附加問句：I never tell lies, **do I**?／…has few friends…, **does he**?
- **too…to…**（太…而不能）→ 用**否定**附加問句（並非完全否定）：You are too young to vote, **aren't you**?
- **不定代名詞主詞**：something/anything/nothing/everything → 附加主詞用 **it**；someone/anyone/no one/everyone → 用 **they**：Something bad happened…, **didn't it**?／Someone took my notebook…, **didn't they**?
- **主要子句＋附屬子句** → 依**主要子句**：If it is fine tomorrow, they will go picnicking, **won't they**?
- **主要子句是 I know/think/believe/guess…** → 依 **that 子句**：I know (that) you don't want to hurt me, **do you**?／I believe he will try his best, **won't he**?
  - ⚠️ **I don't think…**：英文把「不」前移，故視為否定 → 用**肯定**附加：I don't think we can spend so much money…, **can we**?
- **感嘆句** → 用**否定**附加問句、主詞與感嘆句一致：What a kind man he is, **isn't he**?／How interesting this comic book is, **isn't it**?

## 📢 祈使句的附加問句（不適用三原則）
> 祈使句附加問句意思是「**好嗎？**」，用 **will you?** 徵詢同意（用 won't you? 會變成質疑對方意願）。

| 祈使句 | 附加問句 |
| --- | --- |
| 肯定祈使句 | **will you?** |
| 否定祈使句 | **will you?** |
| 邀請祈使句（Have…） | **won't you?**（較客氣婉轉） |
| Let's… | **shall we?** |
| Let us／me／him… | **will you?** |
| Let's not…／Don't let's… | **all right?／OK?** |

- Tell me what to do, **will you**?／Don't turn off the light, **will you**?／Have a cup of tea, **won't you**?／Let's call it a day, **shall we**?／Let us give you a hand, **will you**?／Let's not talk in English, **all right**?

## ⚠️ 易錯點分析

> [!WARNING]
> **常見錯誤（皆為來源整理的重點）**
> - **三原則**：肯否相反、動詞依直述句且**否定附加必縮寫**、主詞用人稱代名詞。
> - **am → aren't I**；**There is → isn't there**（主詞留 there）。
> - **have to** 用 do（一般動詞）；**have + p.p.** 用 have（助動詞）；**he's** 看是 is 還是 has。
> - **否定字**（never／few／nothing…）→ 用**肯定**附加問句。
> - **too…to** → 用**否定**附加問句。
> - **something → it；someone → they**。
> - **I don't think…** → 用**肯定**附加問句（…, can we?）。
> - **祈使句 → will you?（好嗎）**；Let's → **shall we?**。
